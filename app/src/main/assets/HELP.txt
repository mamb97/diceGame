This is a two-player dice game with the first player always being computer (AI).

Step 1: AI rolls two dice consecutively.

Step 2: Next, the human player is prompted to select any number (from the given dice images)

Step 3: Finally, the human player should roll the dice.

To decide the winner, the total scores of each player are calculated by using modulus of "6".

The player with a greater score wins and the game results in a tie when the scores are equal.
